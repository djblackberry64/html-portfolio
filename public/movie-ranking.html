Top Movies of All Time According to Dean

My top 7 Movies of all

Pitch Perfect

Movie Series about Acapella Singing and YA life.

Spirited Away (in German: Chihiros Reise ins Zauberland)

Best anime film I've ever watched. A Ghibli(pronounced: Gi bu ri) masterpiece.

Mulan

A disney film about a woman dressing up as a man to head to war instead of her father to spare her father of the burden of war he can't carry anymore because of his old age.

Interstellar

A great sci-fi movie about companionship, loyality and parallel universes.

John Wick 1

Really cool and emotional Action movie with great stunts and acting.

Rush hour

Movie series with Jackie Chan. With funny but sometimes a little offensive jokes and, action scenes that capture your attention.
